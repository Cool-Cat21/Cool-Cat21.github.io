The Great Plains Travel Guide

Where is it at?

The Great Plains are located west
of the Mississippi River and east of the Rocky
Mountains. You can find them from Texas all the way up to North Dakota.
Here is a list of some other states that have the Great Plains.

  • South Dakota
  • Wyoming
  • Colorado
  • Iowa
  • Minnesota

What is it?

The Great Plains consists of a large area of
flatland that extends for miles and hardly any trees are to be
seen. There is mostly prairie, steppe, and grassland in
the Great Plains. You can find animals like bison and pronghorn in the area.
Some other animals that can be found are...

  • Black-footed ferrets
  • Greater sage grouse
  • Prairie dogs

Personal Thoughts

Some people may think that the Great Plains is
